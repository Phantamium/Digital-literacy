Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

- Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone
- Always check the URL before entering personal information
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible
- Do not respond to messages that create urgency or panic
- Verify emails and messages from official sources before trusting them
- Keep your devices and apps updated for better security

Financial Safety (UPI Specific):
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
- Do not approve unknown payment requests

If you become a victim of cybercrime:
- Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
